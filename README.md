  # Privacy Policy — Numeric Clicker                                                                                                                                        
  **Last updated:** April 20, 2026                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                            
  ## Introduction
                                                                                                                                                                            
  This Privacy Policy describes how Numeric Clicker ("the App") handles user information. The App is developed by Qawa and is available on Google Play.                   
                                                                                                                                                                            
  ## Data Collection
                                                                                                                                                                            
  Numeric Clicker does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. The App does not require user accounts, login, or registration.                                
                                                                                                                                                                            
  All game data (progress, settings, achievements) is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted to any server.
                                                                                                                                                                            
  ## Third-Party Services                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                            
  The App uses the following third-party services:                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                          
  - **Google Play Billing** — for in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy
  policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy                                                                                                                       
                                                     
  - **Expo / React Native** — the App framework. No user data is collected or transmitted through these services.                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                            
  ## Analytics                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                            
  The App does **not** use any analytics or tracking tools.                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                            
  ## Children's Privacy
                                                                                                                                                                            
  The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
